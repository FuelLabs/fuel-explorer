Fluid Protocol introduces an over-collateralized borrowing platform specifically designed for the Fuel ecosystem. Anchored by USDF, the native stablecoin of Fuel, Fluid Protocol facilitates 0% interest-free loans against collateral, leveraging Fuel Network's exceptional speed, security, and scalability. This platform stands out through its unique features such as a fixed minimum collateral ratio, partial liquidations, and a governance-free, algorithmic approach to fiscal policy, ensuring a robust, efficient, and user-centric borrowing experience.
